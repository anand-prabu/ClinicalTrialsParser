Brief title

Neurobiology of Opioid Dependence: 2 - 2
Official title

Neurobiology of Opioid Dependence: 2
Brief summary

The purpose of this study is to evaluate the effects of gamma hydroxybutyric on naloxone-precipitated opiate withdrawal.
